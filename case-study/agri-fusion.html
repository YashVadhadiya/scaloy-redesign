Project Overview

With global food security under threat from resource depletion and climate change, traditional farming methods face increasing strain. AgriFusion partnered with Scaloy to pioneer a tech-first, sustainable agriculture platform.

The mission was to empower farmers by leveraging precision agriculture through IoT sensors, AI analytics, and real-time data to enhance productivity, sustainability, and profitability.
